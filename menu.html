Order Instructions

Ordering Process:

  1. Place your order here.
  2. A minimum order value of 89 PLN is required.
  3. For customer support, please call 792-702-887.
  4. We will promptly confirm your order via email, along with the applicable delivery fee based on your address.
  5. Our delivery service operates from 9 AM to 8 PM, and you can expect your delivery within a minimum of 6 hours from the time of confirmation.
  6. Receive your order conveniently at your specified address and time from our delivery vehicle.

Payment Options:

  1. To confirm your order, kindly make an initial payment of 50%, with the remaining 50% due upon delivery.
  2. We accept payments upon delivery by cash, card, BLIK, or telephone transfer to 5360-775-24.
  3. You will receive an invoice by email as confirmation of your order.
