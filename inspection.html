Shipment Loading & Inspections

Ensure your cargo meets quality standards and safety protocols before it leaves the port. Total transparency from warehouse to vessel.

Preshipment Inspection (PSI)

A systematic inspection of units selected at random from all batches of your order, conducted when your production is at least 80% complete.

  • Quantity & Quality verification
  • Packaging & Labeling compliance
  • Functional & Safety testing
  • Regulatory documentation check

Loading Shipment Supervision

Our inspectors stay on-site during the entire loading process to ensure your goods are handled correctly and secured for transit.

  • Container condition inspection
  • Loading process monitoring
  • Cargo placement & securing
  • Seal verification & recording
